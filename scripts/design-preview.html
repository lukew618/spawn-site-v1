Guide Report

Entry · 04.19.2026
Skwalas coming off mid-day below Roza. BWOs late morning on cloudy afternoons.

Yakima is fishing well below Roza Dam. Water is cold but clear, with fish holding in the seams and at the heads of riffles. Morning temps held around 42°F; expect a bump to 48°F by 2pm.

Swing streamers in the lower river. Dry-dropper rigs through the canyon have been producing, especially with a Pteronarcys up top and a small pheasant tail on 18" of tippet.
